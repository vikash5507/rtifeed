About

RTIFeed is a social platform for sharing RTIs and making authentic information available to all people. You may share RTIs which you have filed or propose a new one in the community. RTIs are categorised by departments, topics and states which makes browsing easier. RTIFeed presents personalized authentic information as per reader's relevance and interests.

The RTI Act 2005 was a big step towards making the citizens informed about the activities of the Government. Unfortunately, most RTIs filed are often not shared. Moreover, majority of the RTI activists are not part of any organisation, they often act alone, moved by anger at corruption and other illegal activities. RTIFeed aims at forming a fearless and powerful community of people who want to bring a change by spreading awareness amongst masses.

It is a path-breaking shift from the traditional news distribution system. Not only does RTIFeed guarantee the authenticity of the information, but also empowers and inspires you to be the source of it.
